North Alabama

The city of florence is a quaint collge town by the river. The people are some of the nicest you will ever meet.


The Food

There is a wide variety of great food in florence. You can go to chicago cafe and get a hotdog. My personal favorite is the barbecue.

The Community

There is never a lack of events going on. Whether that be event put on by orgs on campus or just going to the arcade and shooting pool.
